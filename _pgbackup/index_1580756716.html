About me

I am a student User Experience Design at The Hague University of Applied Science. I have a background in art, specifically illustrations and graphic design. Competent in Adobe PhotoShop, Adobe Illustrator, and Figma. I am an attentive listener, empathetic, easy going, and creative.

I completed a minor in Applied Fine Art at Tampere University of Applied Science in Finland. Here I created a depression simulator RPG game, organized community-oriented art activities and had my work displayed in a beer tasting venue, the Pyynikin Brewery Company.

I am driven by a passion for smooth interactions. I strive to create holistic, engaging and meaningful experiences. My background in art and graphic design drive me to be mindful of every visual detail in a design.
