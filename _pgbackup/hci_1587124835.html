HCI: Human Computer Interaction

Coding, physical prototypes, concepting

Two full weeks of concepting, building, and refining our Artificial Creature. We explored many different types of technology, such as Google Lens, arduino, and Processing. Meet F.R.A.N.C.I.S.

F.R.A.N.C.I.S. is our Fabulous, Rampant, For our final product, we wanted to move more into art territory rather than make something practical. We quickly knew we wanted to make a product that plays with emotion. The fruit of our labour materializes in Francis. He is a fabulous, rampant, artificial and notable creature, intrinsically superb. Francis sees you for who you are and acknowledges you. His body fills with joy and the lights in his tentacles start to dance. However, Francis is a social creature, a true extrovert. He has only one motto in life: the more the merrier. He wants to see you surrounded by your friends. Visit him with your party and his lights will dance more exuberant than ever.
