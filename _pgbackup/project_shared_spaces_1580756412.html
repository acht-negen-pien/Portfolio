Project Shared Spaces

Research, Design

In collaboration with the municipality of The Hague, we were given the task to re-design the atrium of the town hall. The client wanted to make this place more visitor centered. We conducted various types of research on the visitors of town hall. We collected their thoughts, behavioral patterns and experiences and generated user insights. These insights were the fundament for each design decision that we made.

Our final prototype is a greeting system that features Pat, your guide through the Atrium. He gives visitors a warm welcome and accompanies you every step of the way. View our product video here and our client handout below.
