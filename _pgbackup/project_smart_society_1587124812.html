Project Smart Society

Design, SCRUM

In collaboration with the Municipality of Delft, we were given the task to refine the logistics of the municipality’s public space management. In particular, our team designed a new interface for public space inspectors to use while out in the field.

We listened closely to the client’s wishes and the experiences and remarks that the inspectors have with the current system. Based on these insights, we designed an application that updates problem cases in real time, progress tracking, and it allows for quick and easy communication with fellow inspectors and relevant third parties.

Since this course was simultaneously an introduction to SCRUM we delivered our product in sprints and had client meetings every couple of weeks, with extensive feedback and reflection opportunities.

For screenshots, detailed explanation and test videos, visit our portfolio website.
